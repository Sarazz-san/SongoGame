Le Relais (Relay)

Movement Mechanic

The heart of Songo's strategy. When sowing seeds, if your last seed lands in a hole that already contains seeds, you don't stop.

  • • Pick up all seeds in that landing hole (including the one you just dropped).
  • • Continue sowing in the same direction.
  • • Chain multiple relays to cross the board in a single turn.

Grand Slam Rule

Songo is a game of strategic balance, not extinction.

Never "Starve" the Opponent

You are forbidden from making a move that leaves the opponent with zero seeds on their side of the board.

info

The Obligation to Feed

If your opponent has no seeds, you MUST play a move that brings seeds into their territory if such a move is possible.

Grandmaster Tip

"In Songo, the winner is often not the one who captures the most seeds early, but the one who manages the flow of the relay to control the board's distribution. Keep your holes 'fertile' with 1-2 seeds to maximize relay potential."
